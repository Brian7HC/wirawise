About Kikuyu Chatbot

Features

  • Traditional Kikuyu greetings (Thayu)

  • Context-aware responses
  • Culturally appropriate etiquette
  • Session tracking & analytics

Research Based

Built on academic research by Nancy I. Karia-Maina (2014) on pragmatic and linguistic etiquette of greeting in Gikuyu language speakers.
